Game Rules:

Welcome to Random Dream Ball – Your Fantasy Battle League.

You are the owner of a galactic dream-ball team and your management skills will determine your success in the Universe!!!

If this is your first time entering the battle field we strongly suggest you log in as a new owner (with credit card in hand) and provide us with your very intimate personal information so that we can inundate you with expansive universes, galactic features, and pumped-up galactic superheroes.

If this is not your first time playing Random Dream Ball, then click on the existing owner button and let’s begin the FANTASY!!!

It is time to select your team: 

  • You have 50 credits to select an incredible dream-ball team.
  • Use the scroll down menu to view the players available to draft, use them credits wisely as some players have special skills called blockers and bruisers.
    • Blockers – Prevent scoring by lower skilled players.
    • Bruisers – Injure an opponent for 2 rounds, but highest skilled player still scores.
  • Select 6 Battle League superheroes and up to 6 benchwarmers (12 max). Will need them bench warming heroes for when a bruiser knocks your player out for 2 rounds.
  • Now that you selected your team you have time now to review your team’s stats and determine if you need to make any Dream-Ball trades.
  • If a trade is needed highlight the player and send him back to the draft pile
  • Once your confident in your selections let’s begin the Greatest Dream-Ball Battle in the Universe!!!
  • Press “Begin the BATTLE”.
